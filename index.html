About TOPH

Treasures of the Pharaohs (TOPH) is a blockchain-based digital asset inspired by the legendary wealth, gold, and timeless treasures of Ancient Egypt.


For thousands of years, the treasures of the Pharaohs have symbolized prosperity, power, and lasting value. TOPH brings that same spirit into the digital era by combining historical inspiration with modern blockchain technology.


Built on BNB, TOPH aims to become a recognized digital treasure for holders, traders, and supporters around the world.
